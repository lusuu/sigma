With a background in dispensary excellence and a heritage that spans over 100 years, we have invested significantly in building tailored offers to help maximise your retail opportunity.

As a pharmacist, you have many choices to develop your business. Sigma offers a portfolio of branded franchises, Amcal, Guardian, Discount Drug Stores (DDS), PharmaSave and Chemist King or can support you as an independent partner as you strive to attain your business goals.

When it comes to business support, we can tailor a solution to your pharmacy needs in product, marketing, training and development and other related services. At Sigma we’re excited by the opportunities our industry presents.

We are facing the future head on and have reshaped our business so that our products, services and our professional service offers are geared entirely to best support forward looking, progressive pharmacists.
